## What EtherAI-Dental Does

### 1. Overnight Insurance Verification — Every Patient, Every Day

**The problem:** Your front desk arrives each morning with a full schedule and no idea which patients have active coverage. They spend the first 1-2 hours calling payers or manually checking portals, one patient at a time.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental automatically verifies insurance for every patient on tomorrow's schedule — overnight, across all your locations. When your staff arrives, coverage status is already confirmed, flagged issues are waiting in a simple queue, and benefits are summarized in plain English — not insurance jargon.

**What this means for you:**
- Save **8-10 hours per week** of front desk time per location
- Catch inactive coverage **before** the patient arrives, not after the procedure
- Reduce same-day cancellations and surprise billing conversations
- Works with both dental (DentalXchange) and medical (Availity) insurance

### 2. Intelligent Staffing — Fill Shifts Faster, with Better Matches

**The problem:** When a hygienist calls out, your office manager starts a frantic texting and calling process. Finding someone qualified, available, nearby, and within budget can take hours — or the shift goes unfilled.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental's Shift Matchmaker instantly scores and ranks available professionals based on credentials, distance from your specific office location, past performance, availability, and rate compatibility. It presents the top candidates with clear reasoning ("3.2 miles away, 4.8 rating, $55/hr — within your budget") and sends invitations with one click.

**What this means for you:**
- Fill open shifts in **minutes**, not hours
- Reduce no-shows with better-matched professionals
- See staffing status across all locations from one dashboard
- Automatic distance calculation from each office — not your headquarters

---

### 3. Claims Follow-Up — Stop Leaving Money on the Table

**The problem:** Denied and aging claims are one of the biggest revenue leaks in dentistry. Most practices write off thousands per year simply because nobody has time to investigate and resubmit.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental monitors every claim across all locations. It categorizes aging claims (30/60/90+ days), identifies denial reasons, and recommends corrective actions — like flagging a missing prior authorization or a coding error that can be fixed and resubmitted. Nothing happens without your approval, but the research and recommendations are done for you.

**What this means for you:**
- Recover revenue that would otherwise be written off
- Identify denial patterns ("Location 2 has 3x more pre-auth denials than Location 1")
- Prioritize high-value claims automatically
- Maintain a complete audit trail for every follow-up action

---

### 4. Patient Communication — The Right Message at the Right Time

**The problem:** Reminder calls and follow-up messages are inconsistent across locations. Some offices text, some email, some forget entirely. Patients show up unaware of their coverage status or outstanding balances.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental drafts and sends personalized appointment reminders that include the patient's specific location address, verified insurance status, and any outstanding balance — all customized per patient, per location.

**What this means for you:**
- Fewer no-shows with timely, personalized reminders
- Patients arrive informed about their coverage
- Consistent communication quality across all locations
- All messages reviewed before sending — nothing goes out without your team's approval

---

### 5. Credential & Compliance Monitoring — Never Miss an Expiration Again

**The problem:** Managing licenses, certifications, malpractice insurance, and background checks for dozens of contractors across multiple locations is a compliance nightmare. One expired credential could mean legal exposure for your entire organization.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental continuously scans every contractor's credentials across all your locations and categorizes them: expired, critical (under 14 days), warning (14-30 days), and upcoming (30-60 days). It automatically sends renewal reminders and can suspend shift eligibility for expired credentials — protecting your practice.

**What this means for you:**
- **Zero expired credentials** slipping through the cracks
- Automatic renewal reminders — your contractors handle their own renewals
- Compliance dashboard showing credential status across your entire organization
- Protection from liability — no one with expired credentials works a shift

### 6. Revenue Cycle Intelligence — See the Full Financial Picture

**The problem:** Understanding your financial performance across locations requires pulling reports from multiple systems, normalizing data, and spending hours in spreadsheets. Most DSO owners get monthly reports that are already stale by the time they arrive.

**Our solution:** EtherAI-Dental analyzes billing, payments, and verification data across all your locations and surfaces actionable insights: which locations are underperforming, where revenue is leaking, which payers are problematic, and what specific changes would have the biggest financial impact.

**What this means for you:**
- **Real-time financial visibility** across all locations
- Side-by-side location performance comparison
- Identify revenue leaks with specific dollar amounts ("Missing pre-auth at Location 2 is costing you an estimated $45,000/year")
- Actionable recommendations ranked by financial impact
- Executive-level reporting for board meetings and investor updates
