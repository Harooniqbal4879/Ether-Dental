Independent Contractor Validation Process

(Freelance Platforms – Payment Readiness & Compliance)

1) Account Registration & Identity Setup

Before any work or payment, contractors must create and verify their marketplace account.

Common data collected:

Full legal name (as per government ID)

Email address

Phone number (OTP verification)

Country of residence

Profile photo

Physical address

Purpose:

Establish identity

Prevent duplicate / fraudulent accounts

Enable tax & payment compliance

2) Government Identity Verification (KYC)

Most platforms require Know Your Customer (KYC) verification before allowing withdrawals.

Documents requested:

Passport

Driver’s License

National ID card

Residence permit (if applicable)

Verification methods:

ID upload (front/back)

Selfie / liveness check

Video verification (some platforms)

AI facial match against ID

Platforms requiring strict KYC:

Upwork

Fiverr

Toptal

GoTu (healthcare staffing → stricter)

Freelancer.com

3) Tax Documentation (Critical for Payment)

Freelancers must submit tax forms based on residency.

🇺🇸 U.S. Contractors

Form W-9

Legal name

Business name (if LLC)

SSN or EIN

Address

Federal tax classification

🌍 Non-U.S. Contractors

Form W-8BEN / W-8BEN-E

Foreign status certification

Tax treaty claims

Permanent address

Why required:

IRS compliance

1099-NEC reporting (for U.S. payees)

Withholding tax determination

4) Payment Method Setup

Contractors must configure withdrawal methods.

Common payout options:

Direct bank transfer (ACH / SEPA / Wire)

Payoneer

PayPal

Wise (TransferWise)

Skrill

Platform escrow release

Information collected:

Bank name

Account number / IBAN

Routing / SWIFT code

Account holder name (must match KYC)

Validation steps:

Micro-deposits

OTP confirmation

Third-party payment KYC (Payoneer, etc.)

5) Work Eligibility & Compliance Checks

Especially relevant for regulated industries (healthcare, finance, legal).

GoTu / Healthcare Platforms

Additional validations may include:

Professional license verification

NPI number (U.S. clinicians)

DEA registration (if prescribing)

Malpractice insurance

Background checks

Immunization records

CPR/BLS certifications

General Platforms (Upwork, Freelancer)

Usually limited to:

Skills verification tests

Portfolio review

Employment history

Certifications (optional)

6) Contractual Agreements

Before payment eligibility, contractors must accept legal terms:

Independent Contractor Agreement

Marketplace Terms of Service

Escrow & dispute policies

Non-circumvention clauses

Confidentiality / NDA (project-specific)

7) Payment Security & Fraud Checks

Platforms run internal risk reviews:

IP address monitoring

Device fingerprinting

Geo-location checks

Payment fraud screening

Sanctions / OFAC screening

Accounts may be held or suspended if flagged.

Platform-Specific Notes
Upwork

Requires before withdrawal:

Government ID

W-9 / W-8BEN

Payment method

Video verification (if flagged)

Address verification

Pays via:

ACH

Wire

Payoneer

PayPal

Freelancer.com

Requires:

ID verification

Selfie verification

Tax form (for U.S.)

Payment method setup

Escrow system releases funds after milestone approval.

GoTu (Healthcare Staffing)

Stricter due to clinical risk:

Clinical license

Background check

Drug screening (sometimes)

Immunization records

Malpractice insurance

Work authorization (I-9 equivalent validation)

Fiverr

Requires:

Phone verification

Government ID (for withdrawals)

Tax forms (via Fiverr Revenue Card / Payoneer)

Toptal / Premium Networks

Extensive vetting:

Technical interviews

Live coding tests

English proficiency

Identity verification

Contract agreements

Payment onboarding

Payment Release Preconditions (Consolidated Checklist)

Before a contractor can be paid, platforms typically ensure:

✅ Identity verified (KYC complete)
✅ Tax forms submitted (W-9 / W-8)
✅ Payment method validated
✅ Contract terms accepted
✅ Work delivered & approved
✅ Escrow funded (if applicable)
✅ Compliance checks cleared

1099 Compliance Considerations (For Your Internal Process)

If you engage freelancers off-platform or via marketplace:

You must collect:

W-9

Legal name

Address

SSN/EIN

Payment records

And issue:

Form 1099-NEC (if ≥ $600/year, U.S.)
